Who I Am

I’m Jordan Estrada — a problem-solver, a builder, and someone who believes good technology should feel effortless. I study Information Systems with a cybersecurity emphasis at Utah State University, where I explore the relationship between people and the systems they rely on every day.

My work blends technical curiosity with creativity. From cloud-based APIs to websites like this one, I enjoy taking ideas from concept to reality and understanding how the decisions behind the scenes shape the experiences users have in front of the screen.

In the long run, I want to build tools, platforms, and digital experiences that make people say: “I didn’t know tech could be this smooth.”
